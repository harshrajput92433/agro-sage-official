## **2. Scope of the Project**

AgriSage Official focuses on simplifying plant health monitoring through intelligent automation. The project includes:

* Identifying diseases from plant leaf images using a trained deep learning model.
* Detecting healthy vs. diseased leaves across multiple plant types.
* Delivering fast and reliable predictions suitable for mobile and web use.
* Providing a model that is easy to integrate into real-world applications.
* Supporting students, researchers, and developers working in agri-tech.
